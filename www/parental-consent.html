PARENTAL CONSENT FORM I’M OK, MOM

Children under the age of 13 are not permitted to use the “I’m OK, Mom” app (the “App”) without the written permission of the parent or legal guardian.

A parent or legal guardian (“you”) must sign and submit this form in order to add a child under the age of 13 as a Child in the App and allow the child to use the Services.

Once you give your consent for the child to use the Services within the App, we will collect the following data regarding the child:

  • nickname you provide the child with (please, use any nickname not associated with a name and surname of the child);
  • avatar photo if you decide to upload the one (please, use the photo not identifying the child);
  • location data including GPS, WiFi and mobile location (you can turn off the location services on your device at any time) to provide you with the Services;
  • cell phone number to enable the child to send SOS-SMS notices;
  • device usage statistics to display the child’s device battery level.

The information listed above will be disclosed to a person identified as a Parent in the App. In addition, the child will be able to send SOS-SMS notices to a Parent with whom they have the same account.

Also, we share some personal information, including location data, collected from a child under 13 with our third-party providers and companies in the same corporate group, as specified in our Privacy Policy.

We will not use or share the personal information of a person added as a Child within the App, including a child under the age of 13, to any third-party providers or partners for the advertising purposes.
